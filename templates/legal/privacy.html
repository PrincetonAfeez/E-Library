Privacy Policy

Template policy — replace with counsel-reviewed language before commercial launch.

What we collect

Account details (name, email, library card number), circulation activity (loans, holds, fees), and technical logs needed to operate the service.

How we use it

To provide library services, send transactional notices you can opt out of where non-essential, and secure the service. We do not sell personal data.

Subprocessors

Third parties that may process data on our behalf are listed in docs/policies/subprocessors.md (e.g. payment, email, SMS, and error-monitoring providers). Optional AI features run locally and do not transmit catalog or patron data to an external model provider.

Retention & your rights

Returned-loan history is anonymized by default. You can export your data (Export my data) and request deletion. Retention windows are described in our data-retention policy.

Security

Data is transmitted over TLS, passwords are stored using a modern key derivation function, and second-factor secrets are encrypted at rest.
